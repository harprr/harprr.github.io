Budget Report on Innovation and Technology Development

Hong Kong budget report 2018 released on Feb 28 grants 500 billion HKD to Innovation and Technology Bureau(ITB)

in order to promote the development of local high-tech industrial clusters.

The fund will mainly be distributed to the construction of Lok Ma Chau Science Park and the innovation and technology fund.
